How to prepare a perfect Tea...

Tea is one of the most popular and refreshing beverages enjoyed across the world. Prepared by brewing tea leaves in hot water or milk, it offers a soothing aroma and a comforting taste. Tea is commonly consumed in the morning or during breaks, helping people feel relaxed and energized at the same time.

Different varieties of tea exist, such as milk tea, black tea, and herbal tea, each with its own flavor and benefits. Whether shared with friends or enjoyed alone, a cup of tea often brings warmth, calmness, and a moment of peace to everyday life.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton (or vegetables for veg biriyani)
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Onions (thinly sliced)
  • Yogurt (curd)
  • Ginger paste
  • Garlic paste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Cook the meat or vegetables
  3. Prepare the masala
  4. Layer the biriyani
  5. Dum cooking (final steaming)
